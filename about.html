About Us

ACM Student Chapter

ACM Student Chapter is a student body organization supported by the international body ACM. The Student Chapters established by ACM aim to enhance learning through the exchange of ideas among students, and between established professionals and students. ACM Student Chapters exist to serve the computing and information processing community, and the public at the local level.

AU-CEG ACM Student Chapter

The AU-CEG ACM Student Chapter is the ACM Student Chapter of Department of Computer Science and Engineering, CEG, Anna University. There are only 6 ACM Student Chapters in India and AU-CEG ACM Student Chapter is one among them. Its activities include

  • Organizing technical and career workshop
  • Conducting technical meetings and debates
  • Conducting ACM ICPC model programming contests
  • Organization of PRODIGY, the High School Contest
  • Co-ordination of inter-year activities within the department

Association for Computing Machinery

ACM is an international organization which functions as a locus for computing professionals and students working in the various fields of computer science and information technology. ACM promotes research and development in computer science by conducting conferences, maintaining online digital libraries etc. ACM establishes student chapters which provide an opportunity for students to play a more active role in the association and its professional activities.
